A physician executive with over 20 years of experience at the intersection of clinical medicine, informatics, and public health. Currently serving as Chief Medical Officer at Peach Tree Healthcare, one of California's largest Federally Qualified Health Center networks.

Focused on leveraging artificial intelligence and clinical decision support to improve outcomes for underserved communities while building high-performing medical teams and sustainable operational models.
