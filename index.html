About

Executive medical leadership grounded in informatics, operations, and public health.

Dr. Edward Kalpas is a physician executive with more than two decades of experience spanning clinical medicine, informatics, quality improvement, and public-sector leadership. He is board certified in Preventive Medicine, Clinical Informatics, and Lifestyle Medicine.

His work focuses on helping health systems and FQHCs deploy AI responsibly, optimize EHR workflows, and translate complex clinical and operational challenges into measurable improvements in access, efficiency, and quality. He has authored five peer-reviewed publications and held CMO and CMIO roles across hospital systems, public health agencies, and federally qualified health centers.
