About

Executive medical leadership grounded in informatics, operations, and public health.

Dr. Edward Kalpas is a physician executive with more than two decades of experience spanning clinical medicine, informatics, quality improvement, and public-sector leadership. He runs clinical operations for a community health center and builds AI thinking systems on the side, because the next generation of physician leaders needs to speak three languages: clinical, technical, and financial.

His career path: Army physician, preventive medicine residency, public health at Columbia, clinical informatics at ASU, and now a CMO role where he is responsible for quality, provider experience, and operational performance across a multi-site FQHC system. He has authored five peer-reviewed publications and is completing an MBA at Boston University.
